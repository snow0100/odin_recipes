Spinach and strawberry salad

Ingredients

  • 2 bounches of spinach
  • 4 cups of sliced strawberrys
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup of white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar

Steps

  1. Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl
  2. Whisk oil, sugar and vinegar together and pour over spinach and strawberrys
  3. Serve and enjoy!
